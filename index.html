About Real Madrid

Founded

1902

Founded as Madrid Football Club before earning royal recognition in 1920.

Country

Spain

The club represents Madrid and competes at the top level of Spanish football.

Nickname

Los Blancos

The name comes from the club's iconic all-white home kit.

Stadium

Santiago Bernabéu

A historic football venue in Madrid and one of the sport's most famous arenas.

President

Florentino Pérez

Known for leading major eras of global stars and ambitious club projects.

Real Madrid is the most successful football club in the world. Known for its rich history, legendary players, and numerous trophies, the club has become a symbol of excellence in football.
